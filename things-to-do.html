Cities of Taniti

Taniti City

Taniti City is the capital of our small island nation. Most tourists spend most of their time in Taniti City, which boasts native architecture and nearby white, sandy beaches that encircle Yellow Leaf Bay.

Popular activities in Taniti City include:

  • Beachgoing
  • Tours of the city and surrounding rainforest
  • Helicopter rides
  • Art galleries
  • Bowling

Merriton Landing

Merriton Landing is a rapidly developing area on the north side of Yellow Leaf Bay. Cruise ships dock roughly once per week, and the increased tourism has resulted in many new activities for guests to enjoy.

Popular activities in Merriton Landing include:

  • Several pubs, including a microbrewery
  • A new dance club
  • Movie Theatre
  • Arcade
  • Soon to come: A nine-hole golf course!

Excursions

Rainforest & Volcano

Experience the thrill of standing on the edge of a living, breathing volcano. As you venture closer, feel the ground rumble beneath your feet and hear the earth's pulse echoing through the air. Witness the mesmerizing dance of molten lava cascading down the volcano's slopes, creating a dazzling display of vibrant colors against the night sky. It's a sight that will leave you in awe, capturing the essence of the Earth's magnificent power.

Fishing and Diving

Cast your line into the bountiful depths of our crystal-clear waters and experience the thrill of fishing like never before. Whether you're an experienced angler or a novice seeking a new challenge, our fishing tours cater to all skill levels. From trolling for trophy marlins to battling the acrobatic leaps of sailfish, our experienced guides will take you to the best fishing hotspots, ensuring a memorable day on the water. Brace yourself for the exhilaration as you reel in the catch of a lifetime and create stories that will be shared for generations.

Submerge yourself in a vibrant underwater paradise that will leave you in awe. Our diving tours take you to mesmerizing coral reefs teeming with a kaleidoscope of marine life. Explore hidden caves and grottos, swim alongside majestic sea turtles, and encounter schools of tropical fish in their natural habitat. Marvel at the colorful coral gardens that create a dazzling backdrop for your underwater journey. Whether you're a seasoned diver or a first-timer, our experienced dive masters will guide you through unforgettable underwater expeditions.

Remote Beaches

Leave behind the crowds and immerse yourself in the ultimate beach lover's haven. Our remote island beaches offer a haven of solitude, where soft golden sands meet the gentle lapping of turquoise waters. Lounge beneath swaying palm trees, bask in the warm tropical sun, and let your worries drift away with the ocean breeze. These pristine shores provide the perfect escape from the hustle and bustle of everyday life, allowing you to rejuvenate your mind, body, and soul.
